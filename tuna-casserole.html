Depression Era Tuna Casserole

This Recipe

Ever since I was a child this simple yet tasty recipe has brought me so much happiness and over the years it has changed so very little. My mother used to make it every week as a staple of the rotation because its so quick and easy to prepare and the fact that the ingredients are quite cheap probably helped out a bit too. The simple pleasure of biting in to a warm home cooked meal with the family is unparralleled and this recipe will have your family bondng over dinner in no-time!

    Ingredients
  • 1 box Angel Hair or Egg Noodles
  • 1-2 Can(s) Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Salt (~1 TBsp)
  • 1 bag Frozen Peas(Optional)
  • Cheese(Optional)
  • 1 SleeveRitz Crackers(Optional)
      • Steps
  • 1. Fill a pot about 3/4 full with water and add enough salt that the water looks slightly cloudy and bring to a boil.
  • 1a. (OPTIONAL) If you are planning to add cheese or peas preheat the oven to 400F.
  • 2. Add box of noodles, cook until al dente (about 8 mins).
  • 3. Strain noodles and add to serving dish, open your can(s) of cream of mushroom soup and mix together.
  • 3a.(OPTIONAL) If you are electing to add peas, cheese or crackers mix the peas in now with the cream of mushroom soup and add cheese (either mixed in or on top) then crush your ritz crackers and sprinkle over your dish. Place in oven for 10 mins, then remove.
  • 4. Let stand covered for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and then serve!
