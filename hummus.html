Hummus

This Recipe

Bruh this shit is so good!

    Ingredients
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 6 TBSP Tahini
  • Salt 2 TBSP
  • Lemon Juice 2 TBSP
  • Olive Oil 2 TBSP
  • Water 1 cup
  • 2 TBSP Cumin
      • Steps
  • 1. Open can of chickpeas, drain and place into blender. Blend on any setting until they start to become a paste.
  • 2. Combine water and lemon juice in small mixing bowl and mix. While the blender is on add mixture SLOWLY to chickpeas.
  • 3. In a small mixing bowl combine Tahini and Olive oil. Mix and then SLOWLY pour into blender. Mix in blender on any setting, stirring as needed to make sure everything combines.
  • Add salt, pepper and cumin to blend and mix on low stirring as needed until spices are thoroughly mixed in.
  • 4. Scoop Hummus from blender and place into serving dish, allow to rest at room temp for up to 30 minues before serving.
