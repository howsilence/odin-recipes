Restaurant Grade Spicy Mayo

This Recipe

My girlfriend and I love this recipe to the point where we now have high cholesterol and need to lose weight. WE. PUT. THIS. SHIT. ON. EVERYTHING.

    Ingredients
  • Mayo 1/2 Jar
  • Sriracha Sauce 3TBSP
  • Salt ~1 Tsp
  • Dijon Mustard 1-2TBSP
  • Lime Juice 1 TBSP
  • Garlic Powder 1TBSP
      • Steps
  • 1. Scoop Mayo into a mixing bowl, add Sriracha and stir.
  • 2. Add Salt, Pepper, Dijon Mustard and Garlc Powder then stir.
  • 3. Add lime juice and once more mix your ingredients together, then transfer to a serving dish.
  • 4. Cover and refridgerate for atleast 30 minutes before serving.
